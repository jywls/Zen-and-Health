❓ How to Play

🎨 Color Sort

Sort colored balls into matching tubes using strategy and patience.

  • Click a tube with balls to select it
  • Click an empty or matching-color tube to move balls
  • Match all balls by color to complete the level
  • No time pressure—play at your own pace

🖌️ Paint by Numbers

Color numbered regions to create beautiful artwork.

  • Select a color from the palette
  • Click numbered regions to paint them
  • Paint each region with its assigned number color
  • Complete the artwork to move to the next painting

🔗 Untie the Rope

Physically move ropes on a board to untangle complex knots.

  • Click and drag the colored rope ends to move them
  • Ropes automatically snap to the grid for precision
  • Rearrange ropes so they don't cross each other
  • When ropes are untangled, you complete the level
  • Use strategic planning to find the solution

💡 Tips for Anxiety Management

  • 🚫 No time limits—play at your own pace
  • 😊 No fail states—mistakes aren't punished
  • 🎯 Focus on the present moment
  • 🧘 Use deep breathing between moves
  • ⏸️ Take breaks whenever you need
  • 📈 Track your progress and celebrate improvements
